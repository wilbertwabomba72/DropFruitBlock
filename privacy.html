Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Fruit Block Drop handles information when you use the game. Fruit Block Drop is a casual puzzle game for iPhone and iPad.

Information We Store Locally

The game stores gameplay progress on your device, such as unlocked levels, star ratings, best results, and music preferences. This information is stored locally using device storage and is not used to identify you directly.

Advertising and Tracking

Fruit Block Drop may include third-party advertising SDKs, including ad mediation or ad network partners such as Chartboost, Yandex Mobile Ads, Vungle/Liftoff, and InMobi. These partners may process information such as device identifiers, advertising identifiers, IP address, approximate location derived from IP address, ad interactions, app activity related to ads, diagnostics, and device information.

On iOS 14 and later, the game requests permission through Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt before accessing the advertising identifier for tracking purposes. If you do not allow tracking, advertising partners may still show contextual or non-personalized ads where supported.

Security and SDK Configuration

The app may use security or SDK configuration services to help initialize integrated SDKs, protect ad delivery, prevent misuse, and support basic app operation. These services may receive technical information needed for those purposes.

Children's Privacy

Fruit Block Drop is not intended to collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us through the App Store support channel so we can review the request.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information directly. Third-party advertising partners may process data according to their own privacy policies and may use it for ad delivery, measurement, fraud prevention, and related advertising purposes.

Your Choices

You can control tracking permission in iOS Settings under Privacy & Security > Tracking. You can also reset or limit advertising-related settings using the controls provided by iOS.

Data Retention

Local progress data remains on your device until you delete the app or clear the app's data. Third-party SDK partners may retain data according to their own policies.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the date at the top of this page.

Contact

For privacy questions or support requests, contact us at dropfruitblockgame@gmail.com.
